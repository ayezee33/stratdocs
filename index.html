Get Your Leadership On The Same Page

Your leadership team is busy building the product, talking with customers, marketing your software and more. In order to hold them accountable to their work, projects and metrics you need a rock solid plan.

Strat docs allows you to build a process, create a plan, and put it in front of your leaders to make sure everyone is on the same page and executing the right strategies.

Here is how it works

  • Define the outcome of each step of your planning process
  • Connect your systems and tools to support these outcomes
  • Assign your leadership team todos and keep them accountable

Build & Document Your Strategic Plan

There is no one-size fits all strategic plan. There are a lot of factors to consider when building your plan. But one thing is constant, you have to have a process for how you build your plan.

Strat Docs allows you to organize your process into easy to follow steps to ensure they are hit on time. If you are struggling with a process, we have a built in template that will work for most SaaS companies in the $10k - $10mm ARR range.

Start with our template or create your own and design the outcomes each step needs to have. Once the plan is in place, it's time to get leaders looking at it weekly.

Give Your Operations Team A System

SaaS operations teams are forced to use software and spreadsheets to keep everyone on the same page. It can be difficult to make sure data is flowing through the business. Stop updating spreadsheets and docs that no one is looking at or finds useful.

Strat Docs system brings everything into one place. Your leadership team can look at the simple dashboard every morning to see how your company is progressing towards it's strategic plan.
